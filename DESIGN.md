# DESIGN.md

## Mood
Mid-morning writing desk — crisp white paper, crimson ink stamp, quiet focus.

## Color strategy
Restrained. Pure white surface; crimson primary ≤10% for actions and selection.

## Palette (OKLCH)
- bg: oklch(1 0 0)
- surface: oklch(0.975 0.004 28)
- ink: oklch(0.22 0.02 28)
- muted: oklch(0.48 0.018 28)
- primary: oklch(0.489 0.19 28.3)
- accent (SQL): oklch(0.48 0.11 230)

## Typography
Geist Sans (UI) + Geist Mono (SQL). Product scale, not display.

## Layout
Mobile: bottom tabs + full-screen editor. Desktop: sidebar + list + editor.
Safe-area insets for iPhone notch/home indicator.
